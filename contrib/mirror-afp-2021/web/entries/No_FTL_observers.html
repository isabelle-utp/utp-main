No Faster-Than-Light Observers

 

Title: No Faster-Than-Light Observers
Authors: Mike Stannett (m /dot/ stannett /at/ sheffield /dot/ ac /dot/ uk) and István Németi
Submission date: 2016-04-28
Abstract: We provide a formal proof within First Order Relativity Theory that no observer can travel faster than the speed of light. Originally reported in Stannett & Németi (2014) "Using Isabelle/HOL to verify first-order relativity theory", Journal of Automated Reasoning 52(4), pp. 361-378.
